1. Target business rules

Admin Portal in product language = role PortalAdmin (lookup id 2010).

  1. 1. PortalAdmin full control: PortalAdmin can Create, Update, Delete any question; Activate / Deactivate / Archive; Approve (→ Public visibility); and Reject (rejection reason required).
  2. 2. Create: SchoolAdmin, CampusAdmin, Teacher, and Parent can create questions. Create stamps SchoolId / CampusId from the creator (when present). Status is always PendingReview (no Draft).
  3. 3. Update own (pre-approval): SchoolAdmin, CampusAdmin, Teacher, and Parent can update their own questions only while status is not Approved (PendingReview, Rejected).
  4. 4. Delete own (pre-approval): SchoolAdmin, CampusAdmin, Teacher, and Parent can delete their own questions only while status is PendingReview or Rejected (not Approved).
  5. 5. Delete after Approved: Once Approved, only PortalAdmin may delete. Non–PortalAdmin cannot delete Approved questions (even their own).
  6. 6. Activate / Deactivate / Archive: Only PortalAdmin can Activate, Deactivate, or Archive questions. No other role.
  7. 7. View Approved (scoped): Manage roles see own questions plus Approved questions visible in their scope:
    • Public — everyone (all schools/campuses)
    • School — anyone in the same school (all campuses of that school)
    • Campus — anyone in the same campus (SchoolAdmin also sees Campus-approved items in their school)
    Students cannot open the question bank — they only see questions inside quizzes.
  8. 8. Approve / Reject = three admin roles (scoped):
    • CampusAdmin — approve/reject PendingReview in their campus → visibility Campus (usable within that campus)
    • SchoolAdmin — approve/reject PendingReview in their school → visibility School (usable across the school’s campuses)
    • PortalAdmin — approve/reject any → visibility Public (usable by everyone)
    Reject requires a non-empty rejection reason. After Reject: owner may edit and resubmit to PendingReview, or delete.
  9. 9. Excel import (Web only): Import from Excel is available on Web for create-capable roles. Always creates PendingReview. Not available on Mobile.

Rule numbers match the product request (item 6 was unused in the source list).

9. Open decisions

  1. Import always PendingReview (Status column ignored). Never Approved via import.
  2. Delete while linked to quizzes: blocked for everyone including PortalAdmin (implemented).
  3. Rejected edit stays Rejected until Submit for review (implemented).
  4. IsAiApproved dual gate dropped — Approve + Visibility sets quiz eligibility in scope (implemented).
  5. Fill in the Blanks is NOW — accepted answers. Descriptive remains NOT NOW.
  6. Fill AI/Teacher review flags apply at attempt submit. Bank approval is 3-tier (Campus / School / Public).
  7. Promotion of Campus→School→Public on already-Approved items: not required for v1 (approve from PendingReview only). Optional later.
